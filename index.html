Bjorn Stabell

Serial entrepreneur and technologist. Building AI products and open-source developer tools.

About

Co-founded App Annie / data.ai (app market intelligence), Happylatte (mobile games), and Exoweb (agile dev services, 90 people). Running companies since 2000 across Beijing, Vancouver, and now Palo Alto. Also co-created XPilot, one of the first multiplayer internet action games (1991).

Currently building AI products and open-source infrastructure for the terminal: Silvery (React terminal UI framework), Flexily (pure-JS flexbox engine), Termless (headless terminal testing), and Loggily (debug + structured logging).
