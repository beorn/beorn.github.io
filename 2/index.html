about

I write software for the command line — tools that stay out of your way and do exactly what you need. My current focus is the terminal UI layer: making it fast, composable, and testable.

Most of my open-source work lives in the terminal ecosystem: a React-based TUI framework, a pure-JS flexbox engine, a headless terminal library for testing, and a universal color theme system. On top of that I'm building km — a knowledge machine for agentic knowledge workers.
